About IIESP - International Institute of Education & Skills

IIESP is a universal platform dedicated to providing world-class vocational training, skill development, and professional education. We empower learners with practical competencies needed to excel in today's fast-paced, globally competitive world.

Our comprehensive programs span multiple disciplines including IT & AI, Healthcare, Hospitality, Engineering, Design, Agriculture, Automotive, and Green Energy. With expert faculty, modern facilities, and industry partnerships, we bridge the gap between education and employment.

We are committed to developing skillful professionals and entrepreneurs who can positively impact their communities and beyond.

  • Expert faculty with industry experience and academic excellence
  • State-of-the-art facilities and modern learning infrastructure
  • Internationalperspective with diverse international student community
  • Career support including resume workshops, placements, and counseling
  • Flexible learning options: classroom, online, internships, and apprenticeships

Our mission is to deliver world-class education and training that develops competent professionals ready to lead industries, drive innovation, and create positive social impact globally.
